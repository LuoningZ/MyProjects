  • AUDIO

    I wrote a story which happened in the midnight. On a silent road, a woman ran rapidly, with some more crying. Final back to home, she started looking for the key from her bag. She found the key, opened the door in panic, and quickly closed the door,and continue to breathe. Footsteps getting closer and closer, and finally stopped outside. Peng, peng, peng! Loudly. After a while, peng, peng, peng. Still loudly. After a while, the door was kicked out, with the woman screaming,and muttered: No, no, no! Boom! A gunshot. Woman's voice stopped immediately, the body fell heavily. The footsteps appeared again and walked steadily toward the door, getting smaller and finally disappeared, accompanied by the wind blowing the door sound.

  • MAX

    In my patch, I can adjust any colour I want, then output the colour’s RGB data, and I also used two types of sounds to be the output. Using the scale object to input the three original data and then make the value become larger to make sure the final sounds would be larger and easier to be heard. And see the graph separately and listening to the sounds they have, it would give people a direct feeling to the colours.
